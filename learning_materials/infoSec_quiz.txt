Title: Information Security Basics

Question: What is Information Security (InfoSec)?
Answer: Protecting important data from being stolen, misused, or destroyed

Question: What does the 'C' in the CIA Triad stand for?
Answer: Confidentiality

Question: What is Application Security?
Answer: Protecting programs and the data they handle

Question: What is Cryptography?
Answer: Utilizing code to protect messages only the person meant to receive can read

Question: What is the goal of Disaster Recovery in InfoSec?
Answer: To get systems up and running after sudden events
